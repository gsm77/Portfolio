About

The Forth collective is a part of the Joint Oceanic Nations
that covers the stretches of North sea and Edinburgh.
The people of the North Collective are so proud and
fond of their culture that they have preserved
the heritage and ideologies into various aspects
of their lives like the clothing, currencies etc..

Currency

Coins

Coins of cred values 5 and 10 exists.
The coins represents the moon and the sun respectively
The half and whole concept of coins were inspired by
the viking system of money where silver was cut into
fragments to produce coins of lesser value.

Bank notes

Notes of cred values 100 and 500 exists.
Both the notes follow a particular dominant
colour temperature. The 100 creds is inspired by the North sea
whereas the 500 cred's colour palette is inspired by the
infamous flora of the region, the thistle plant.
Both the notes have celtic influences on their appearance.

Cards

Bank card

The bank cards are made of bio luminnescent material
that makes them glow in the darkest and deepest areas of the sea.
This makes them easy to be found and suitable for the environment.

C-MIST card

The C-MIST cards are used for travel purposes within
The Complex-Maglev International Subsea Transport network
The card has a biometric sensor behind in the shape of a dome
Like the bank card, the C-MIST card also illuminates in the dark.
